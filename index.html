📸 Instagram Guidelines

  • Times in UTC format "10-13" means post between 10:00 and 13:00 UTC
  • The first hour of the window is usually the strongest
  • Content mix per week: 3 Reels + 1 Carousel + 1 Image post
  • Reel length sweet spot: 45-90 seconds
  • Always add subtitles from frame 1 (for sound-off viewers)
  • Every Reel needs a DM-share CTA — "Send this to..."

📘 Facebook Guidelines

  • Times in UTC format "07-10" means post between 7:00 and 10:00 UTC
  • The first hour of the window is usually strongest
  • Bangla + Urdu: 60-90s long-form videos (best retention)
  • Arabic + Uzbek: 15-30s short clips (retention crisis — shorter helps)
  • All FB videos need strong thumbnails
  • Post vertically (9:16) — all new FB videos are Reels by default

🌍 Time Zone Quick Reference (convert UTC to local)

  • Pakistan / Uzbekistan = UTC + 5
  • Bangladesh = UTC + 6
  • Iran = UTC + 3:30
  • Saudi Arabia = UTC + 3
  • India = UTC + 5:30
  • Indonesia (Jakarta) = UTC + 7
  • Russia (Moscow) = UTC + 3
  • Germany = UTC + 1
